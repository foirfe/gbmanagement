ABOUT GB BRAND ACTIVATION

How we work with Brand Activation

We don't really use the word sponsor, because to us, you are partners. We take the partnership with your brand very seriously. Without the partners that we work with, the athletes couldn't do what they do, and on that level. Me, and the team around me, do everything we can to make the athletes lives as easy as possible, so they can focus on getting results. By doing that, we have the best prerequisites for representing your company in the best way possible.

At GB Management we are happy to be working with a solid list of partners. We take pride in representing the brands with the utmost respect. Be it on social media, sharing content with our partners, or representing your company, when we give it our all, in races across the globe.
