ABOUT ME

I'm Nikolaj Grønbek Ohlhues. I started GB Management in 2018, but my passion for the sport of triathlon started many years before that. Growing up I competed with my dad and brother and was part of Frederiksund Triathlon that arranged events in my hometown. In 2018 I finally had the chance to go after my dream job. So, I did. I've since been able to get a great team of freelancers around me, that helps with everything from branding to content.
